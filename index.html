The Dragon's Tavern

Mission Statement - The mission of the Dragons Tavern is to create an inclusive and creative community of Dungeons and Dragons players from all walks of life and experience levels that want to enjoy the Dungeons and Dragons universe in a welcoming environment.

Club Information - We meet in the Millenium Center Room 125 from 6-8pm every Wednesday night! Anyone is welcome to join at any point, we provide game supplies and snacks.

About Us

Our club represents the community of Dungeons and Dragons enthusiasts at East Tennessee State University. We meet to discuss D&D, develop characters, and organize campaigns. Welcome new & seasoned players alike.
